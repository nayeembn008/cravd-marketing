The future of food is homemade.

Everyone knows they should eat better. So why don't they? It's not laziness. It's the lack of time, knowledge, and access to quality food. We're changing that. The future of food is personalized. And homemade. Get Cravd.

How it all started

Cravd was born from the shared experience of two college mates who became dads, realizing the struggle busy families face in finding time, energy, or even interest to cook daily meals. Cooking is becoming a heavy burden. Yet, every household deserves personalized, healthy, home-cooked meals.

Every day, our dedicated home chefs visit customers' homes to prepare meals that not only nourish but also cater to each family's unique preferences, ensuring everyone enjoys the comfort of a personalized home-cooked experience.

Values that we strive for

The Day We Knew We Had to Do Cravd

At Legoland with our kids, we overheard a lively conversation between homemakers about meal solutions they’d tried—meal kits, home chefs, restaurant orders. "I've tried them all," one said. "The novelty wears off, meals aren't personal, and there's so much waste." Another added, "Hiring a chef sounds great, but coordinating is a hassle, and hourly rates are unpredictable."

Then came the magic words: "Why isn't there a platform to book home chefs online?"

That sealed the deal for us.
